Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Full name, email, age (18+ verification), gender, profile picture, location.
  • Account Data: Login credentials, password changes, account deletion requests.
  • User Content: Stories, posts, chat messages, live stream interactions.
  • Usage Data: App activity, device info, ad interactions (via AdMob).
  • Payment Data: Purchase records via Google Play Billing (handled securely by Google).
  • Device Permissions: Camera, microphone, photos/media/files access for app features.

2. Use of Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain app services.
  • Verify age and eligibility.
  • Process payments and in-app purchases securely.
  • Display personalized ads through trusted ad networks.
  • Communicate with you regarding account and support.
  • Improve and optimize app performance and user experience.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and Firebase Analytics to analyze app usage and personalize content. You can manage or disable cookies and tracking through your device settings.

4. Data Sharing and Security

  • We do not share your personal data with unauthorized third parties.
  • Data is secured using industry-standard encryption (SSL) and Firebase security rules.
  • We share data only with trusted service providers as needed to deliver app functionality (e.g., payment processors, ad networks), all under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. User Controls

  • You can update, edit, or delete your profile and personal data at any time.
  • Account deletion requests are processed within 24 hours.
  • Privacy settings allow you to hide your age, location, typing status, and other personal information.
  • You have control over notification preferences and ad personalization settings.
